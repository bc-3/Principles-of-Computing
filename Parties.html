    Party Trends Over Time

    This is a graph of total mass shootings that occured at parties over the years. From this we can see that from 2018-2021 shootings at parties has risen drastically. This may be a result of more firearms being present at these parties, or it somehow might be related to Covid-19 since the pandemic started in 2019. Either way whatever is causing this trend must be adressed and should be something to keep track of for party goers.
